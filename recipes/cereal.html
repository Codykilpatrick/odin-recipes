Cereal with milk

A breakfast item as old as time itself, cereal

The breakfast cereal has been a staple in all diets from children to adults. It is essential, requires minimal cleanup and is tasty.

Ingredients

  • Milk of choice
  • Cereal of choice
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Instructions

  1. Place bowl on table
  2. Pour favorite cereal into bowl
  3. Pour milk over ceral evenly
  4. Allow time for soak
  5. Enjoy!
