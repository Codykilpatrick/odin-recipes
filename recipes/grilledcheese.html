Poor mans grilled cheese

This is the easiest way to make an edible grilled cheese

This recipe originate from my father who does not have a cullinary bone in his body. But he tried his best.

It's not great but you will have a grilled cheese in less than 60 seconds!

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of any type of bread
  • One or two slices of cheese
  • A toaster
  • A microwave

Instructions

  1. Pull out all ingredients
  2. Toast bread for prefered crisp
  3. Put cheese inbetween toasted bread, microwave for 15 seconds
  4. Complete!
